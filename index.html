Hi, I'm Seongyoon Kim, a Ph.D. at Multi-Physics Modeling and Computation (MPMC) Lab of Yonsei University, Republic of Korea. At present, my research focuses on developing digital twins based on electrochemical and data-driven models for the diagnosis and optimization of energy systems. Additionally, I am currently studying: 1) physics-inspired machine learning, 2) uncertainty quantification (Bayesian inference) and numerical optimization for robust parameter identification, and 3) graph-based causality analysis.
