Hi, I'm Seongyoon Kim, a Ph.D. candidate at Multi-Physics Modeling and Computation (MPMC) Lab of Yonsei University, Republic of Korea. My research focuses on the electrochemical and data-driven modeling for battery diagnosis and prognosis – including machine learning & deep learning, uncertainty quantification, and metaheuristic optimization.
